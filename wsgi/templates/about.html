This is a free web app developed & maintained by Ashish Nitin Patil.
It is exclusive to Mu Sigmans (official email id needed to register).

Mu Katta is NOT an official website of Mu Sigma!

Mu Katta is a simple attempt at easing the following problems...

  • Want to share a cab to airport?
  • Hunting for a room-mate or a new place to move in? (coming soon...)
  • Want to carpool to work? (coming soon... maybe)

Privacy & ToC...

It's a helpful attempt at easing the pain of my fellow Mu Sigmans.
Even though the website is Open Sourced, it is very secure.
No third party (non Mu Sigma email ids) can view website data
Even I cannot view your plaintext passwords
For legalities, refer to the project licensing.

Contact me...

For feedback/suggestions, email me at ashishnitinpatil@gmail.com
Please mention "Mu Katta" in the subject line!

Technical details...

The web app is powered by the Open Shift cloud platform by Red Hat.
It runs on Python & uses the Django web framework.
The "pretty"ness of the site is due to Bootstrap 3 & django-bootstrap3 utilities.
